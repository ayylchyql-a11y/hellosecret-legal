Hello Secret

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Privacy by design

HelloSecret is built to collect as little as possible. It is an anonymous space for sharing a thought, being understood, and letting it go.

What we do NOT collect

We never collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location. Sign in with Apple gives us only a stable anonymous identifier — we never receive your name or email.

What we store

A random anonymous name, the date you joined, the secrets and replies you post (text only), your empathy reactions, the users you block, reports you send, and your language preference.

Content moderation

Every secret is checked by automated keyword and AI moderation before it appears, to keep the space safe. Moderation processes only the text you choose to publish.

Service providers

We use trusted infrastructure providers to operate the app: Supabase (database, authentication, and hosting; servers located in the European Union) and Anthropic (AI content moderation). Text you publish is sent to these providers only to store it and to check it for safety before it appears. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising.

No social graph

There are no followers, profiles, direct messages, or public user pages. Nothing about you is built into a profile others can browse.

Your content is public to readers

Secrets and replies are visible to other users until they close. Please don't share information that could identify you or anyone else.

Reports, blocking & safety

You can report any content and block any user from the post menu; reports stay anonymous. We operate a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive users — reported content is reviewed and offending accounts are removed, typically within 24 hours. Content suggesting someone may be in danger may be escalated for priority review.

Your rights, retention & deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app, which removes your profile and all of your secrets, replies, and reactions. You can also contact us at any time to ask us to remove specific content or to exercise your data rights.

Children

HelloSecret is not intended for children. Please follow the minimum age shown on the App Store listing.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions, a safety concern, or a data request? Email us at ayylchyql@gmail.com.
